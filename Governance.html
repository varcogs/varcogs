Governance & Impact



Our belief is that Learning and Development spans the entire employee life cycle and therefore an integral part of an organization’s growth and a strategic advantage.


Our capability spans the process of systematic discovery of individual and organizational developmental needs, to learning strategy, design and execution supported by concurrent impact assessments and the program governance. It is a continuous cycle driven by goals and designed for impact. Each organizational requirement is independent and calls for the application of a custom solution.

A typical process starts with identifying current and future business needs and challenges, and distilling these to learning and development needs. This helps us measure impact of learning and development on business and organization growth.
