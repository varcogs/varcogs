At VARCO, we are professionals with a multi-disciplinary background who are clear and committed about the scope and quality of services to be innovatively created and delivered to our clients.


Vijay Kumar

Founder-CEO at Varco Global Solutions


Vijay Kumar is responsible for the firm’s strategy, team and operations across the global network. The mission is to deliver the best client results in our stated space.

Before setting up Varco, his career has been with HCL, ITC, Sterling Holiday Resorts, Extract (a CRM venture), Aptech Software, NIIT and the India Today Group (ITG), in significant leadership roles with exposure to global markets with Government, Industry and the Social sectors.

Over the course of around 30 years, he has participated in Policy Advocacy and Influence especially across countries, industry partnerships and alliances. He has represented India with industry lead delegations and engagements to Africa, CIS, APAC, BRIC’s.

His professional exposure across multiple businesses, with multi-site operations in over 40 countries across diverse and complex global regions in Asia, EMEA, CIS, LATAM, provides a depth of experience in dealing with business challenges.

Vijay has done his MBA from Calcutta University and Executive Management from IIM-C.
